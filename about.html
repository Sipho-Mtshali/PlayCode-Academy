About PlayCode Academy

Empowering young minds to think like coders before they ever touch a computer.

Our Story

PlayCode Academy was founded by Sipho Mtshali with a vision to make coding education accessible, fun, and meaningful for young children. Recognizing that traditional coding education often starts too late and focuses too much on screens, Sipho developed the unique Play-Code-Think model.

Our approach introduces coding concepts through physical games, teamwork, and problem-solving activities that children naturally enjoy. By starting with unplugged activities, we build a strong foundation in computational thinking before children ever interact with computers.

Our Mission

"Empowering young minds to think like coders before they ever touch a computer."

Our Vision

"To make coding education fun, inclusive, and problem-solving oriented for every child."
